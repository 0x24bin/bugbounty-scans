DHS Advantage, a partnership between the Department of Homeland Security and the General Services Administration, is a commitment towards improving the acquisition process for DHS customers. 

DHS Advantage has been designed to enhance your procurement experience by providing a streamlined, efficient purchasing system for procuring high quality commercial products, services and solutions.  This online system provides robust tools for performing market research, as well as a streamlined ordering process for procuring products and services.

Please note that access to DHS Advantage is limited to Department of Homeland Security users.

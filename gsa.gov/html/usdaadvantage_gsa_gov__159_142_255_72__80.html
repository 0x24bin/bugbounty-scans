  Welcome to the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) virtual marketplace, known as USDA Advantage.  This partnership between USDA and the General Services Administration (GSA) is part of USDA's Integrated Acquisition System (IAS) "e-Alliance" initiative, an effort to better leverage USDA spending power to obtain better pricing, ordering methods, and delivery terms for commonly acquired items and services.

Unlike other Internet-based shopping sites, this site is designed especially for USDA personnel. USDA Advantage includes USDA discounted Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPAs) negotiated for USDA users.  These lower prices can help you stretch your budget further to better deliver your organization's mission to the American public.

USDA personnel are reminded that USDA Advantage is for purchases in support of USDA official business only. Personal purchases are prohibited. All purchase card and procurement policies remain in full force and effect. USDA Advantage contains a broad range of products. Only items for which there is a legitimate business need shall be acquired.

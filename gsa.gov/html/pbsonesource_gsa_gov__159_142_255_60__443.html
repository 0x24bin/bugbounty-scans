PBS OneSource is a "One GSA-One Voice" strategic approach to integrating and improving the delivery of the product and service offerings of GSA's Public Buildings Service and Federal Acquisition Service.

PBS OneSource:
Provides easily accessible contract vehicles to support the delivery of PBS products/services
Supports PBS goal of consistent service delivery across the nation, by customizing contract vehicles to best serve PBS' needs
Provides an easy to navigate, single point of entry to the real estate related solutions sets offered by FAS

To use, click on the category below that best meets your contract needs. If you do not see an appropriate category then you can enter your own customized search in the box below.

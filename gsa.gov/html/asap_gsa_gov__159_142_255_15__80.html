Welcome to the GSA Advantage Spend Analysis Program (ASAP). ASAP is a centralized reporting tool designed to give authorized users access to GSA Advantage sales and statistical data. Reports are designed to return data results based on user determined report time period (i.e. dates), procurement tools (i.e. schedules or stock) and additional criteria (i.e. socioeconomic factors).

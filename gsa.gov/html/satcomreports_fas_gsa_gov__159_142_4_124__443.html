GSA SATCOM Report Portal

Welcome to the GSA SATCOM Report Portal. The purpose of this portal is to provide a single point of entry for contractors to submit their Quarterly and/or Monthly SATCOM Reports. This portal is for both IT Schedule 70 Enhanced COMSATCOM Quarterly Reports for SINs 132-54 and 132-55, and the Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (ID/IQ) Contracts CS2 and CS-SB. Reports shall be submitted in accordance with the terms and conditions of the specific SATCOM contract awarded to each company.

Note: The GSA SATCOM Report Portal does not replace the 72A Quarterly Report System used for the Federal Supply Schedule contracts. Please continue to follow the procedures required for 72A Sales.

For assistance with the GSA SATCOM Report Portal, please contact us at satcomreportsupport@gsa.gov .

VSC Help Desk

What we do

The VSC maintains a staff ready to assist vendors in the preparation and submission of electronic catalog files to GSA Advantage!, and in the submission and reporting of sales data.

When can you contact us?

Vendors may contact the VSC staff Monday - Friday, 8:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m. Eastern Time, at (877) 495-4849, or via email at vendor.support@gsa.gov.

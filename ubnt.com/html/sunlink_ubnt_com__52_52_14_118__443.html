Opportunity Shines Bright

Now a full plug and play solar system solution is available to the world allowing independent contractors to quickly establish a solar installation business.

A Complete Deployment Solution

sunMAX™ hardware offers advantages including simplified installations for reduced deployment time, optimal performance using microinverters, railless mounting, and integrated equipment kits.

Solar Panels Microinverter Solar Gateway Mounts & Accessories

Solar Panels

sunMAX Solar Panels can be installed on rooftops in different layouts: portrait, landscape, or a combination of both to produce up to 260W of solar power.

Microinverter

sunMAX Microinverters are 95.5% efficient, safely converting DC power from solar panels to AC power. Available in two models (US and EU) to meet standards worldwide.

Solar Gateway

The sunMAX Solar Gateway communicates with each Microinverter in the array using Bluetooth and then sends data via Ethernet across the customer LAN for monitoring support.

Mounts & Accessories

Customized array mounts secure the solar panels to the roof structure. Different rooftop types, including tile and composition, are supported.

sunMAX Design Apps & Monitoring

Use the intuitive sunMAX™ software to enter the location information, design a system for the customer’s roof, create a customer proposal, apply for a permitting package, and generate a complete bill of materials for ordering equipment.

MonitoringAs an operator, you can create and manage all sites from design to installation. Customers can only monitor their own sites.

StatisticssunMAX software reports hourly, daily, monthly, or yearly energy production levels using clear graphs that are easy to read.

Education

To educate you on the fundamentals of the solar field, including the sunMAX platform, Ubiquiti offers a free online training course. You can also become certified as a Ubiquiti Solar Residential Specialist upon completion of course requirements.

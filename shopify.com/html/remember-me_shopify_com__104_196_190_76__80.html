Shopify Pay

Save your information for a fast and secure checkout on stores powered by Shopify.

Easy setup on your first purchase

  1. Opt-in on your first purchase

    Simply click “save my information” and enter your mobile phone number.

How it works for future purchases

  1. Enter your email

    Start typing your email address at checkout like you normally would.

  2. Enter your authorization code

    If we recognize your email address, you will automatically receive a text message with a code to authorize the purchase.

  3. Complete your purchase

    Just review your information and complete the purchase.

Frequently asked questions

  • What is Shopify?

    Shopify powers over 500,000 online stores around the world as a trusted commerce provider.

  • What information is saved?

    Only your shipping details and payment information are stored and encrypted on Shopify’s PCI compliant servers.

  • Who has access to my saved information?

    No merchant has access to specific payment information. Once you verify your identity, the info moves from this environment directly to the payment network to complete the transaction.

  • Can I make a purchase if I don’t have my phone or if I changed my number?

    Yes, you can always fill in your information by entering it manually.

Opting out

You can opt-out of Shopify Pay’s accelerated checkout feature anytime. When you opt-out, all of your saved information will be deleted.

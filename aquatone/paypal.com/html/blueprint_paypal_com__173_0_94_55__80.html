  • What are the eligibility criteria for Startup Blueprint?

    Your startup passes our eligibility criteria if it:

    • Is less than five years old
    • Is privately held
    • Makes less than $3M USD of annual revenue
    • Focuses on making mobile and/or web apps, software, or hardware
    • Is a member of our partner incubator, accelerator or VCs portfolio
    • Is not an alumni of Startup Blueprint
  • Can I also receive Braintree benefits?

    Your startup must operate out of a US, Australian or European-based office. You must also have a bank account with a US, Australian, or European chartered bank. If Braintree is available in your country, then you can enable Braintree benefits.

  • How is what we’re offering different from other startup programs in the tech industry?

    We are the first payments-focused company to offer a startup program dedicated to helping startups grow their business.

  • Are startups already using PayPal and Braintree eligible for this program?

    Of course, all startups are eligible for this program if they meet the eligibility criteria.

  • How can I get my incubator, accelerator or VC on Startup Blueprint?

    Please have your incubator, accelerator, or VC complete our application form.

  • Is there a specific plan for Braintree/PayPal to take equity or purchase startups from Startup Blueprint?

    We did not create Startup Blueprint to specifically acquire or take equity in startups, but rather to support and foster the startup community.

  • Is there a limit to the amount of transactions that are eligible for free processing? What about the type of transactions?

    There is no limit for the number of transactions, but there is a limit for the total amount of the benefits:

    • PayPal benefit: $50,000 USD of free PayPal fees (or up to 18 months whatever comes first)
    • Braintree: $100,000 USD of free total payments volume (or up to 18 months whatever comes first)

    Once startups reach one of those limits, they will start receiving PayPal’s and Braintree’s standard fees. Terms apply.

  • I tried to login for the first time but with no success. What can I do?

    Please, recheck your credentials, clear all of your cookies and then try again.

  • I have logged in successfully and I am getting a “You’re part of Startup Blueprint” page. What does this mean?

    The resulting page confirms that you have successfully on-boarded for the Blueprint program and that you are now eligible for the Blueprint benefits.

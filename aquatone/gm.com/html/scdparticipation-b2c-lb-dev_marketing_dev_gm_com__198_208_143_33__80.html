Thank you for your interest in participating in Shop Click Drive (SCD). We are excited you have chosen to begin your onboarding process. In order to get started, we ask that you please follow the four easy steps below.

Get Started

How does it work?

Step 1

Please read the Shop Click Drive Dealer Participation Agreement (DPA) and check the box to agree to the terms.

Step 3

Please select a trade-in book estimation tool, read the RouteOne Addendum Agreement for Vehicle Values Services (Trade-In Book), and check the box to agree to the terms..

Step 2

Please read the RouteOne Shop Click Drive (SCD) Amendment and check the box to agree to the terms.

Step 4

Fill out the Dealer Information section. All fields are required, unless otherwise noted. If you agree to the terms, then press the 'Submit' button to start your onboarding and launch process. Once submitted, you will also receive an email with the DPA attached for your records.

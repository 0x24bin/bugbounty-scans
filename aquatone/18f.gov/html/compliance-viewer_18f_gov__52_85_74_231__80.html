Welcome to the Compliance Viewer!

The Compliance Viewer aims to make the Authority to Operate (ATO) process and regulatory compliance more tangible, actionable, and transparent for everyone involved.

To do this, it automates the parts of the compliance process that can be automated — namely, scanning web applications — and helps with those that require a human touch. As the project evolves, its scope will expand to include much more than app scanning. For more on what we’ve got planned, check out our backlog (ZenHub extension required).

If your project appears on the list below, click its name to view the latest compliance results. If it’s not on the list, click the New Project? link to add it. For a walk through of the ATO process, visit the Before You Ship guide.

United PerksPlus℠ Special Offer: Newark - Buenos Aires Double Points Promotion

 
For a limited time, United PerksPlus℠ member companies earn double points when eligible travelers fly qualifying flights on United®on our new nonstop service to and from New York/Newark to Buenos Aires, beginning October 28, 2017.

We know you have many choices for air travel, and we appreciate your company’s loyalty to United PerksPlus℠.

We would like to offer you one more reason to choose United for all your company’s travel needs: earn double points on all qualifying flights with United when traveling on the new nonstop flight between:

  • Newark, New Jersey (EWR)
  • Buenos Aires, Argentina (EZE)

Program details

Simply register by December 15, 2017 in order to maximize your company’s ability to earn extra points. Registration for the promotion must take place prior to ticketing and must be completed by December 15, 2017. To register, please provide your account ID, name, email address, and your company’s name. We will take care of the rest!

• Register for the points promotion by December 15, 2017
• Ticket qualifying trips between September 30, 2017 and December 15, 2017
• Fly between October 29, 2017 and February 28, 2018

Thank you for choosing United, and we look forward to seeing you on board soon.

Offer terms and conditions

  1. Only original recipients of the offer email invitation (authorized representative and company contact of active United PerksPlus account) may register and participate in the promotion.
  2. To qualify for this offer, authorized representatives and/or company contacts of active United PerksPlus accounts must register through the promotion website http://unitedperksplusoffer.united.com/, then purchase and complete paid qualifying flights on United and/or United Express® during the valid ticketing and travel periods. Registration for the promotion must take place prior to ticketing and must be completed by December 15, 2017.
  3. Ticketing dates: September 30, 2017 – December 15, 2017
  4. Travel dates: October 29, 2017 – February 28, 2018
  5. Members can earn double award points on flown qualifying flight segments in published fares in F, A, J, C, D, Z, P, Y, B, M, E, U, H, Q, V, W, S, T, L, K, G or N fare classes. Certain specialty tickets, including but not limited to unpublished, consolidator, group/tour, government fares and opaque fares do not qualify for this offer. Tickets with corporate discounts (excluding CPA Plus) from United and other United PerksPlus participating carriers are not eligible for this offer.
  6. Qualifying segments for Double Points promotion are qualifying flights to and from:
    • Buenos Aires, Argentina (EZE) and Newark, New Jersey (EWR)
  7. Double award points is defined as two award points for each Qualifying Revenue dollar multiplied by applicable hub multiplier that would otherwise be credited in connection with paid segments flown during the promotion period. Qualifying Revenue is defined as actual flown revenue, excluding all taxes, service fees, and refunds.
  8. For this offer, a “segment” is defined as one takeoff and one landing.
  9. A "qualifying flight" is defined as one-way true origin and destination flight, which may have more than one segment.
  10. The offer is not valid on United-marketed codeshare flight segments operated by other carriers that are not participating carriers of United PerksPlus.
  11. This offer is not combinable with other offers.
  12. Double award Points will be posted to the member's United PerksPlus account six to eight weeks after the end of the promotion travel period.
  13. Service charges for ticketing with a United representative in the 50 United States, District of Columbia and Canada are $25 by phone, $30 at a City Ticket Office and $50 in person at the airport. Ticketing charges may vary outside of the U.S. and Canada and based on MileagePlus status level. The ticketing service charge does not apply for tickets purchased on united.com. Service charges are subject to change.

Discover United PerksPlusSM

Welcome to United PerksPlus, a complimentary corporate program designed to reward small- and mid-sized enterprises for business travel on United®, United Express®, All Nippon Airways (ANA), Austrian Airlines, Brussels Airlines, Lufthansa and Swiss International Air Lines.

With these world-leading airlines, you have even more to discover:

  • The most comprehensive global route network, including world-class international gateways to Africa, Asia, Australia, Europe, Latin America and the Middle East with nonstop or one-stop service from virtually anywhere in the United States
  • Austrian Airlines, Brussels Airlines, Lufthansa and Swiss International Air Lines maintain one of the world's largest and most modern fleets, with over 650 aircraft
  • ANA, Japan's premier airline, offers award-winning inflight service and a comprehensive global network
  • United hub airports in nine cities, including the four largest cities in the United States

Points-to-Miles Conversion: The world of the MileagePlus® program at your fingertips

In March 2017, United PerksPlus and the United MileagePlus program introduced Points-to-Miles Conversion, an industry-leading new reward option that offers the ability to convert your company-earned points into MileagePlus award miles and deposit them into your eligible employees’ individual MileagePlus accounts.

Consistently voted the world's Best Frequent Flyer Program by Global Traveler magazine, MileagePlus offers innovative ways to earn and use award miles, from flights in United's global network to once-in-a-lifetime experiences and the latest in entertainment.

Sign in to your account now and explore the opportunities to convert points to miles.

Complete reward program rules may be found in the United PerksPlus terms and conditions

Please note: Points-to-Miles Conversion is not available to Earnings Plus customers.

PerksPlus NavigatorSM: Discover a deeper connection

At United, we believe that your company’s increased commitment to United PerksPlus demonstrates a loyalty that deserves recognition. That is why we introduced PerksPlus Navigator, a new program level for our most frequent business travelers.

Companies must track $400,000 of eligible Qualifying Revenue during the current term in order to qualify for PerksPlus Navigator. The more your employees fly United and our participating carriers, the greater likelihood of earning PerksPlus Navigator status in the program.

The PerksPlus Navigator program features:

  • 15 percent more points for eligible qualifying flights
  • Enhanced quarterly reporting that provides details on travel spend and redemption patterns
  • Additional redemption options, such as MileagePlus Premier® Platinum status for your travelers

To see if your company qualifies for PerksPlus Navigator status, sign in to your account now

Complete program rules may be found in the United PerksPlus terms and conditions

Please note: This program is not available to CPA Plus or Earnings Plus customers.

Discover how it works

To qualify for enrollment into the United PerksPlus program, your company must have at least five employees and a valid Tax ID, and not be enrolled in any other United or participating carrier program that provides a discount. Simply fill out the United PerksPlus Enrollment Form to get started.

See the United PerksPlus terms and conditions for more information

Automatic renewal

United PerksPlus is an annual program and your membership will automatically renew.

Customers in the U.S. and Canada
Once your company's eligible revenue totals $25,000 USD on United, United Express, Lufthansa Group and ANA (where applicable) within the 12-month period immediately after enrollment, we will automatically renew your membership. Upon renewal, outstanding points earned during the prior term will remain valid for an additional 24 months.

Customers in Latin America
Once your company's eligible revenue totals $15,000 USD on United, United Express and ANA within the 12-month period immediately after enrollment, we will automatically renew your membership. Upon renewal, outstanding points earned during the prior term will remain valid for an additional 24 months.

Customers in the Caribbean and Central America
Once your company's eligible revenue totals $10,000 USD on United, United Express and ANA within the 12-month period immediately after enrollment, we will automatically renew your membership. Upon renewal, outstanding points earned during the prior term will remain valid for an additional 24 months.

Redeeming Points

Customers in the U.S. and Canada
Once your company’s eligible revenue totals $5,000 USD for three consecutive months during the first 12 months of your membership, you can begin to redeem points online. Your request will be processed within two business days of submission.

Customers in the Caribbean, Central America and Latin America
Once your company’s eligible revenue totals $3,000 USD during the first 12 months of your membership, you can begin to redeem points online. Your request will be processed within two business days of submission.

Access your account online anytime

Once enrolled, select "My United PerksPlus Account" and log in using your account number and PIN/password to:

  • Manage travelers: Update your registered business traveler list
  • Change PIN/password: Your account security is very important and along with your account number, your PIN/password is the key to your United PerksPlus account. You may change your PIN/password online.
  • Edit account profile: Change your company profile information, such as company contact name, address information, email address or phone number.
  • Redeem your United PerksPlus points: Once your company has enough points to qualify for a reward, the company’s authorized representative may redeem points.

Luxury vacation packages

There’s no limit to the luxury you’ll discover when you travel with United Vacations® Luxury. Whether glamorous or rustic, relaxed or high-energy, the possibilities are endless but the vacations are singular. Our expert staff will answer any questions you may have during the planning process, and will be available to you at any time during your vacation should you need help. Ask, and we’ll make it happen.

Based on their extensive knowledge and years of travel planning experience, our Travel Advisors have hand-picked what we believe to be the best selection of hotels, restaurants and experiences in luxury destinations throughout the globe. But these dedicated experts do more than provide recommendations. Whether it’s a simple getaway or a once-in-a-lifetime journey, they will work with you to craft an itinerary filled with special touches, life-changing experiences and unforgettable memories.

 

Need more reasons to book your luxury vacation with us?

  • The convenience of online travel planning combined with the expertise of our dedicated team of Travel Advisors.
  • Unparalleled customer care before, during and after your vacation.
  • Flexible payment plans let you reserve your vacation for one low deposit and pay the rest at your convenience.
  • Industry-leading Premier Vacation Protection that protects your travel investment, provides you with our Best Price Guarantee, and allows you to change or cancel your reservation for any reason with no penalty.

Welcome to Cisco Hosted Collaboration Solution from AT&T

Admin Support Portal

 


Welcome to the Cisco Hosted Collaboration Solution from AT&T Support Center, a one-stop resource for administrators and end users to find current training, product quick tips, support documentation, downloads and additional information. Our goal: to streamline your AT&T unified communications experience.

UC Voice

This feature-rich, cloud-based IP telephony solution provides easy-to-use, high-quality voice and UC features as-a-Service. You’ll find FAQs, guides, How-Tos, video tutorials and more.

UC Clients

AT&T offers downloadable software components to integrate and extend your unified communications experience with third-party clients such as Cisco Jabber® or Microsoft® Lync®.

UC Federation

AT&T UC Federation is a highly secure intercompany unified communications ("UC") collaboration service that interconnects UC solutions from many of the leading UC Vendors. Federation provides a common platform for interconnecting a Customer's unified communications system(s) with that of their large and small business partners.

Admin Services Portal

Your login access to comprehensive support for UC Services. Chat with a UC Services support specialist, submit a trouble ticket, provide feedback and more.

End User Self Help

Your login access to capabilities that support your UC Voice experience. You can update your single number reach, set up your phone, reset your PIN, consult user manuals and more.

User Adoption Program

Visit this area for customizable assets to help you introduce UC and the benefits it provides to your enterprise. This content is organized into three phases: pre-launch, launch and post-launch.

Related Services

AT&T provides robust services to enhance your unified communications experience, including audio and web conferencing, Telepresence, VoIP, MPLS/VPN, IP Flexible Reach and Business DSL.

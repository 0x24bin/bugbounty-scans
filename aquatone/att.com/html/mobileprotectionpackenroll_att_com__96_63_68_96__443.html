Multi-Device Protection Pack Program Details

You have 30 days from activation or equipment upgrade to add Multi-Device Insurance to your account.

Monthly Charge:

$34.99 (Multi-Device Insurance included)

Covered Incidents:

Protects your device against loss, theft, accidental physical or liquid damage, and mechanical and/or electrical malfunction after the manufacturer’s warranty period has expired.

Standard Deductible:

Device Tier A: $25; Device Tier B: $75; Device Tier C: $150; Device Tier D: $225; Device Tier E: $299
Multi-Device Insurance for non-connected devices are charged a non-refundable deductible for a repair ($89) or replacement ($199).To see a full list of eligible devices and their deductibles go to att.com/mdpp. Declining Deductible discount: Continuously enrolled customers who go claim-free for 6 consecutive months or more can receive 25% to 50% off the standard deductible.

Claim Limits:

6 total claims within any consecutive 12 months with a maximum device value of $1500 per occurrence. Claims from Multi-Device Insurance that are less than 12 months old will be carried forward and applied to the Claim Limits of a subsequently issued AT&T Mobile Insurance policy.

Replacement Device:

As soon as next-day* device replacement for connected devices. Claims may be fulfilled with new, AT&T Certified Restored or other models of like kind and quality. To learn more about the AT&T equipment certification process, visit att.com/mdpp. Colors, features and accessory compatibility are not guaranteed.

Repair of non-Connected Devices:

Multi-Device Protection Pack devices which are not connected to the AT&T network (Wi-Fi® laptops and tablets) are provided a repair or replacement (at the option of the Program Administrator). Devices will most often be repaired within 3 to 5 business days from the date Asurion receives the equipment.

Covered Equipment:

Phone – Includes wireless device, standard battery, standard battery charger and SIM card. If part of the covered loss, choice of one of the following accessories: carrying case, automobile cigarette lighter adapter or standard wired earpiece (not specialty earpiece such as Bluetooth®)

Notebook – Includes wireless device, standard battery, standard battery charger and SIM card

Tablets – Includes wireless device, standard battery charger, USB cable and SIM card

Laptops - Includes laptop, standard battery and standard battery charger

Cancellation Policy:

You may cancel your optional insurance coverage at any time and receive a refund of your unearned monthly premium/charge. We may cancel or change terms by giving you prior written notice as required by law. Any unearned premium/charge will be refunded to you.

AT&T ProTech support:

Requires qualifying device and data plan (list of supported devices is available at att.com/mdpp). Data charges may apply. Functional computer and broadband internet connection required in some cases.

AT&T Protect Plus:

To use AT&T Protect Plus, your phone must be powered on, have text messaging capability, and be within the AT&T coverage area. Service availability, timeliness or accuracy of location is not guaranteed. AT&T Protect Plus is only available on compatible phones. Go to att.com/mdpp. for a list of compatible phones and limitations. Service is provided by Asurion Mobile Applications, Inc. Certain features, such as alarm & lock, may not be available on all devices. Wireless data usage charges may apply. Coverage, device, and technical limitations apply.

Go to att.com/mdpp for additional details.

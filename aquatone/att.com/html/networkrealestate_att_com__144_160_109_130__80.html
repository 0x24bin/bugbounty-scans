FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I have submitted a request for possible tower placement on my property. Will I be contacted?

Due to the volume of unsolicited offers for land and leasing each month, we are unable to respond to all requests. You will only be contacted if your land/building or site meets the criteria of AT&T’s radio frequency engineering and coverage studies and approved capital build plans.

PAYMENTS:

How do I setup direct deposit/Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)?

Please provide either one of the following: bank letter with account informaiton, signed landlord letter with bank information for individuals or letter on company letterhead for business entitites, or a blank voided check with current W-9 form to establish/update direct deposit. Once we receive the documentation, check, and IRS W9 we will be able to begin the process of setting up EFT.

How do I submit a change to the address where my rent payments are mailed:

If only the payment address is changing, you are requested to submit a payment direction form and the most recent dated IRS W-9 form.

How do I have a check reissued?

If your check was lost, stolen, or damaged, please contact AT&T Tower Asset Group Lease Administration (TAG-LA) via telephone at 1-877- 231-5447 option # 3 or via email to AT&T Tower Asset Group Lease Administration (TAG-LA): releaseadmin@att.com with Subject Line-(Payment Reissue Requested-FA#_____). This process may require an updated W9 and Payment Direction Form.

I have not received my rent payment.

Checks are scheduled for delivery by the first of each month. If you have not received your check by the 10th of the month, a stop payment may be processed at your request and a replacement check will be issued. Please contact AT&T Tower Asset Group Lease Administration (TAG-LA) via telephone at 1-877-231-5447 option #3 to request stop payments, reissue, or inquire about direct deposit.

**Please note due to heavier mail volume during holidays and seasonal periods, payments may be delayed due to the United States Postal Service delivery and schedules.

Should I submit a rent invoice?

Rent invoicing is not required and actually discouraged. As a courtesy and if at all possible, please suppress future invoicing of current rent. Rent is paid through your Lease agreement. No copies of Rent invoices are required in our office for issuance of rental payments.

W9

To ensure the security and safety of our vendors and that we maintain the most updated information pertaining to the vendor, the Accounts Payable department will only setup new vendors OR make changes to the payee information for existing vendors with the latest dated IRS W9. Please complete this form with tax classification, EIN or social security number, signature, and date. You may refer to the IRS website or the W9 instructions for further clarification: http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw9.pdf

California Form 590

California Sites and Vendors must provide a CA590 in addition to other forms needed for changes to vendor information. In order to comply with the CA STATE INCOME TAX requirement, we are required to withhold 7% of the payment unless we have either Form 587 or Form 590 completed as appropriate. **Please note: A W-9 form cannot be accepted in lieu of one of the required California forms.

Am I allowed to split my tax parcel?

You MAY NOT split or cause the tax parcel that AT&T leases from you to split, unless our lease agreement with you expressly provides for splitting of our leasehold from your parent parcel.

What if AT & T Mobility is required to pay taxes directly to the Tax Authority?

You are responsible for ensuring all Taxing Authorities in your jurisdictions (Treasurer, Collector and Assessor) have the correct address for mailing tax assessments and statements. It is imperative that you INCLUDE the correct parcel Identification number (PIN) to ensure timely receipt and payment.

I have had past issues receiving tax reimbursements (as prescribed in my lease agreement). How can I be sure that AT&T is going to honor the tax-related obligations of my agreement?

AT&T takes great pride in ensuring that our tax obligations (if prescribed in a lease agreement) are effectively administered and accurate payments are made on a timely basis. If you have a question about the accuracy of your tax reimbursement, please call the toll free number at 1-877-231-5447 option #3, option 2. (Please note that all requests are prioritized as received). For security purposes, you will be required to provide your Fixed Asset Number (FA#).

Why is a W8-BEN form required?

Individuals or companies outside of the US are subject to a 30% withholding tax on income they receive from US sources. However, there are exemptions so you need to fill out the W8-BEN form to:

  • establish you are not a US person/business;
  • claim you are the ‘beneficial owner’ of the foreign income;
  • confirm you are a non-resident claiming exemption from the 30% (if you are eligible)

I received a “B Notice Letter” in the mail. What is this and what action is needed?

Per the CP2100 B Notice letter that AT&T received from the IRS, the vendor listed below has been placed on payment hold. Essentially this means, the tax information on file does not match the IRS database. Some discrepancy exists between the information you provided on your W-9 and the IRS system. AT&T is required to withhold 28% of all payments owed to the vendor and remit it to the IRS until the correct tax reporting information is received and verified. Submit a copy of the latest dated IRS W9 to AT&T Tower Asset Group Lease Administration (TAG-LA) for processing.

THIRD PARTIES

I am the management company or calling on behalf of the Landlord. How do I receive information or payments for a site?

If you are a Management Company or Agent for the Landlord, please provide (signed or on company letterhead) documentation showing the owner has given you authorization to receive rental information and/or added you as contact for site. (You may also provide Legal documentation which shows that you are successor in interest to Landlord if this is applicable.) Without this authorization and for the protection of our Landlords, we are not able to nor obligated to provide information nor redirect payment for the site. Payments are redirected from Landlord by following change of ownership guidelines.

What types of legal documentation or authorization is accepted to receive information for a site if you are a person other than Landlord?

Legal forms of authorization accepted are:

  • Landlord and Management agreement signed and dated by both parties
  • Landlord letter of authorization and/or
  • Power of Attorney

PROPERTY

What is a Fixed Asset/FA #?

A fixed asset number or FA# is comparable to your site account number. The fixed asset # helps to identify your site and allows the representative handling your issue to look up all information related to your account. It should be provided on all correspondence to AT&T Tower Asset Group Lease Administration (TAG-LA).

How do I find my Fixed Asset/FA #?

You may find the FA# on your lease documentation or on a previous check stub in the Invoice column of the check. It is an 8 digit number that begins with a 1******.

How do I request a Change of Ownership?

Please submit all of the following documents:

  • Payment Direction Form (PDF)
  • IRS W-9 - Please submit the most recent dated IRS W-9 form which can be downloaded from our website home page or the IRS website http://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw9.pdf Legal ownership documentation (i.e. deed, assignment and assumption, Bill of Sale, etc.) must also be included.
  • Legal documentation showing proof of ownership (i.e. deed, assignment, Bill of Sale, etc.). If ownership has transferred several times, please submit all documentation showing changes to the current request for ownership.
  • California Form 590 (required for all of the following: sites located in California and businesses operating in or located in state of California)

*If this change is for more than one payee, please include a Payment Direction Form and W-9 for each company and/or individual person. Please ensure to include what percentage (33% / 33% / 36%; 100%; 50%/50%; 80% / 20%; 5% / 45% / 25% / 25% etc.) each payee should receive.

*If this change is due to an inheritance and/or will, please provide the legal documentation showing proof.

*If this change is due to a death, please provide a copy of the death certificate.

***If this change is for multiple sites, please provide one Payment Direction Form and one updated W-9 and include a written request that lists each site. You will still be required to provide the legal documentation listed above for each site.

***If you are a Management Company, please provide legal documentation showing the owner has given you authorization to receive rental payments in addition to the Payment Direction Form and an updated W-9 from the owner.

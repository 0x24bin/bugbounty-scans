The Reward Process

  • Watch your mailbox! You will receive reward claim instructions within 4 weeks of installation/activation.
  • IMPORTANT: You must claim your reward by the expiration date shown on your reward notification.
  • After claiming your reward and meeting your 30 days* active service requirement, most customers receive their reward within 3 weeks.
  • At the AT&T Reward Center you can track the status of your reward any time with the

*30 days or other applicable time period, if any. Check your service offer for details.

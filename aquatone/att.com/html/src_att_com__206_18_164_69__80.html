Welcome

The AT&T Security Research Center was created by the AT&T Chief Security Organization to invent the secure future of modern communications and entertainment.

Our researchers work on very large scale problems in dynamic areas such as mobility and cellular, cloud computing, networking, and data mining. In particular, we search for ways to leverage the power of the network for new security architectures and mechanisms.

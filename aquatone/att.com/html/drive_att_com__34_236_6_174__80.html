Discover AT&T Drive

AT&T Drive is a multifaceted connected car cloud-based automotive services delivery platform that offers infotainment and telematics services, branded interfaces, simplified modern design patterns, standardized application framework and set of RESTful APIs to enable best in class, unified experience for production and prototype vehicles.

When extending capabilities beyond provided demo apps and services AT&T Drive supports developers with free standardized tools and coaching needed to learn to develop and deliver content for vehicles through AT&T Developer Summits and hackathon events.

Natural voice command & control

Natural spoken commands are more and more a part of our lives — from advanced personal computers and smartphones, to home automation — and help to prevent distracted driving. Natural voice commands are built into the core of the platform and have the ability to intelligently grow to meet the latest consumer demands. Custom voice commands are enabled and mapped to specific services of your choosing to further advance your branded experience.

In the car

AT&T Drive's user experience objective is simple: Provide a fully brand-able look & feel, personalized features, standardized tech stack, in a device agnostic, ubiquitous content delivery platform across multitude of form-factors, synchronized through the cloud.

Demo apps provide a great start to developing a fully custom vehicle cloud service. Bringing new apps and content providers is just as easy.

Unified User Experience

Each product has its own UI identity, but they all tie in together for an enjoyable, integrated experience that makes it easy to create and manage accounts, sign up for and use wireless networks in the car, or use a number of apps on the car dashboard.

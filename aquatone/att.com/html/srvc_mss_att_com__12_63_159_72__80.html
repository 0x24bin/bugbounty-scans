Technical Support

If you are experiencing a service effecting technical problem please call the AT&T support team to submit a trouble ticket
at 877-677-2881. You must enter your customer PIN and select option #3 for AT&T Secure E-Mail Gateway.

If you have a general service question or related inquiry, please first refer to the training modules and service documentation
provided through this support site.

Frequently Asked Questions

Setup Question:

What web browsers can I use with the Portal?
Answer:

The following web browsers are supported:

  • Safari/Webkit
  • Opera
  • Firefox/Mozilla
  • Google Chrome
  • Internet Explorer - version 10 and above

Disabling your browser's popup blocker and setting your monitor's screen resolution to 1024x1080 or above is recommended.
Question:

Can the portal be used from a mobile device, such as a tablet, phablet or smart phone?
Answer:

We are not supporting mobile applications, at this time.
Question:

Will I be able to use the Portal while using my Apple Macintosh computer?
Answer:

As long as your computer can run one of the supported browser applications listed above, you should be able to use the Portal.
Using the Portal Question:

What are the hours of operation?
Answer:

The Portal will be available 7 days a week from midnight to 10:00 p.m. Central Time.
User Profile Question:

Who should my coworker contact to obtain access to the Portal?
Answer:

Your coworker can send an email to Communities@att.com to request access. Please include Name, E-mail Address, Telephone Number, the Contract Number(s) and Property Names to be accessed.

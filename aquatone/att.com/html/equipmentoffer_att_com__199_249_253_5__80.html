Frequently Asked Questions
How do I know if I’m eligible for a reward?
A. Within four weeks of activation or installation of your qualifying AT&T High Speed Internet service you can expect to receive a letter in the mail giving you further instructions about the reward process and eligibility.
How do I redeem my reward?
A. You’ll receive notification in the mail within 4 weeks of activation of installation of your qualifying AT&T High Speed Internet service giving you further instructions about the process. The letter will direct you to the AT&T Equipment Reward website and may provide you with a Redemption ID to simplify the redemption process. When you get to the AT&T Equipment Reward website, simply enter the Redemption ID or your AT&T High Speed Internet account number and follow the simple steps to redeem your reward. It’s that easy.
When can I redeem my reward?
A. Your reward will be available for redeeming at the AT&T Equipment Reward website. You’ll know it is there when you receive your notification in the mail.
Do I have to redeem my reward by a certain date?
A. Yes, your reward does expire. The letter you receive will have that date for you.
Whose name will appear on the AT&T Reward Card?
A. The name on AT&T Reward Card will be the same as the name on your AT&T bill.
May I provide the AT&T Reward Card number as payment for my AT&T bill?
A. Yes, if you pay your bill over the phone or online. You may use your AT&T Visa Reward Card for purchases anywhere Visa debit cards are accepted. Please don’t send the AT&T Visa Reward Card in with your mailed payment.

Build smart devices with Google

Google’s Internet of Things (IoT) solutions make it easy to build connected devices. Whether you want to connect to Google services or need a turnkey hardware solution, we provide you with a way to create secure, innovative products.

Android Things (formerly known as Brillo) is perfect for developers looking for a turnkey solution for building devices. It provides you with an OS built on the power, ease, and security of Android and certified hardware to get you from prototype to production quickly. Because it's based on Android, you get access to Android APIs, Google services, and Android developer tools.

Android Things Developers

The Android Things Console provides tools to access your IoT device and perform actions, such as product configuration. After you configure your product, you can provide end users with device information.

Look for the badge.

The Google Partner badge shows that specific Partners have passed Google AdWords product certification exams and are up to date with the latest product knowledge. In other words, they earned it.

Get the help you need.

Google Partners can specialize in one or more of the following AdWords product areas:

  • Search Advertising

    These Google Partners can help you create and optimize ads that show up on Google Search. They can also advise on keyword strategy and budget planning.

  • Mobile Advertising

    Partners certified in mobile advertising can help you connect with potential customers on mobile devices through text, image, video, and HTML5 ads.

  • Video Advertising

    Need assistance with implementing and optimizing your video ad campaigns on YouTube? These Partners can help.

  • Display Advertising

    These Partners can help you create compelling visual ads that reach potential customers on over two million websites and 650,000 apps.

  • Shopping Advertising*

    Certified Partners will help you place your products on Google Search, set up your inventory on Merchant Center, and create Google Shopping campaigns.
    * The shopping advertising certification is not available in some languages. Please check the availability on our Help Center.

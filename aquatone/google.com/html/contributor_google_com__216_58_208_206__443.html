How it works
You load your pass with $5. Each time you visit a page without ads, a per-page fee is deducted from your pass to pay the creators of the website, after a small portion is kept by Google to cover the cost of running the service. The price per page is set by the creator of the site. You will be informed in advance if a site creator changes their price per page. Contributor is easy to update: change settings and add sites or remove them from your pass at any time.

Simplify your life with one service
Maintaining individual subscriptions with each website can be expensive and time consuming. The Contributor pass works with your Google account to remove ads from the participating sites you've selected. Simply sign in and browse the web as usual. You only pay for the pages you view without ads.

Google in Health

Google is making significant investments in health, wellness, and life sciences. Here are some of the teams focusing efforts in this space:

  • Google Cloud
    • Apigee Health APIs
    • G Suite for Healthcare for HIPAA compliant workloads
    • Google Cloud Platform for HIPAA compliant workloads
    • Google Genomics
  • Google Fit
  • Google Search (e.g., health cards, symptom search)
  • Google Research (focused on healthcare applications)

No Contracts, No Lock-in

Moonami is truly open source, backed with an open business model.  Cancel your Moodle Hosting account at anytime from your account dashboard. You’re free to download your entire Moodle™ site and take it with you.

Secure Amazon AWS Cloud Hosting

Your Moonami instance will be protected by multiple levels of security guarded by the same tools trusted by Fortune 100 companies.

Client Moodle sites are provisioned on Amazon’s AWS US East FedRamp Compliant, high-performance, multi-tier fault tolerant, auto-scaling cloud hosting architecture.   Each site is deployed with its own partitioned data directory and privately credentialed database.   All sites completely managed and monitored by Moonami’s Cloud Support Engineers 24x7x365 with full system snapshots every hour, 24/7/365

24/7 Support

Our operations staff works around the clock to ensure your Moodle™ and Totara site is running smoothly. Account holders can send tickets, call, and engage in live chat directly to our support engineers for speedy resolution.

Scalability

Your site can be instantly upgraded to support growing numbers of students, parents and faculty. Whether you have 100 students or 10,000, we have the computing power to meet your needs.

Moodle Migration

Moonami provides complimentary Moodle site migrations and can transition your existing Moodle install over with ZERO interruption or downtime. We upgrade you to the latest release of Moodle with your choice of plugins/integrations.  It is an exact replica of your existing Moodle instance including integration with your SIS, authentication, Moodle plugins, and choice of themes.

Mobile Device Ready

Your Moodle™ site will be pre-configured with a mobile ready theme ensuring that iOS and Android users can use the system from day one.

Expert Services

Our Services team is available to help you maximize your institution’s adoption of Moodle™ or Totara.  Clients are assigned a dedicated project manager throughout the lifetime of the Moonami relationship to help with SIS/CRM/ERP integration, authentication, eCommerce, migration, and of course, fanatical support.

Moodle Totara Site Theming

Our Services team can easily customize the look of Moodle™ or Totara site to match your institution’s website or other branding needs.

Moodle Totara Learning Analytics

Moodle / Totara come with equipped with powerful visual learning analytics and reporting to offer insights into learner progress and ensure that learner objectives are being met.  Analytics can ensure students/learners are engaged or are at-risk with instant notifications on any activity that takes place in the LMS.
